Precision Medicine Based sets of Artificial Intelligence Models for Predicting Surgeries Requirements in Non-Small-Cell Lung Cancer Patients.

    Non-small-cell lung cancer (NSCLC) patients often develop Complications, and the overall survival for these patients is usually perishing. However, a model with high accuracy for predicting the Surgeries Requirement is still lacking. Here, We Canary Global Inc. Data Science Team aimed to establish models based on artificial intelligence for predicting the Surgeries Requirement in NSCLC Patient.
